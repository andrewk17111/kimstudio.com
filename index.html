"Let me take this opportunity to introduce you to Tae Kwon Do. Tae Kwon Do is an internationally recognized sport, yet it is different from other sports in the respect that its mental aspect is as much a part as its physical aspect."

"Through many hours of training, anybody can learn the skillful art of physical techniques, philosophical understanding of life and ability to overcome difficulties in everyday life. "Tae Kwon Do emphasizes mental and physical discipline. The training will help you to build a new personality and a new body."
