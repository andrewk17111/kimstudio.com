PA Icons Exhibit

In November of 2015, the State of Pennsylvania recognized the contributions of Grandmaster Il Hoi Kim to our communities by including him in the State Museum's Pennsylvania Icon's exhibit. Grandmaster Kim's consistent and single-minded dedication to teaching traditional Tae Kwon Do has touched thousands of Pennsylvanians' lives since he opened his first studio in 1974. His mindful instruction and mentoring continues to better Pennsylvania and the Mid Atlantic Region one person at a time.
